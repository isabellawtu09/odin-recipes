The best burger patty recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • Half a teaspoon of salt
  • Half a teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Half a cup of fine dry bread crumbs

How to Make Basic Beef Burger Patties

  1. Begin by whisking the egg, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl. Then, add the ground beef and bread crumbs before forming the mixture into Â¾-inch patties. Place the patties on a preheated grill and cook for six to eight minutes per side.
  2. Make sure the beef's temperature reads at least 160 degrees F before serving, and you're ready to go. Top with classic condiments like mustard, ketchup, and mayo for full-fledged flavor.
